Legal

Privacy
Policy.

1. Data We Collect

We collect your name, phone number or email address, and optional preferences (group size, event preferences) solely for the purpose of managing your event booking. We also collect IP address and UTM parameters for fraud prevention and analytics.

2. How We Use It

Your data is used exclusively to confirm bookings, send event reminders, and provide event access. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties except our vetted service providers (Twilio for SMS, SendGrid for email, Stripe for payments, Supabase for storage) under strict data processing agreements.

3. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3. Sensitive fields (phone numbers) are hashed using bcrypt before storage. Payment details are handled exclusively by Stripe and are never stored on our servers. We conduct quarterly security reviews.

4. Retention Policy

Booking data is retained for 90 days following the event date, then permanently deleted. Analytics data (anonymized) may be retained for up to 12 months for business improvement purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, email privacy@velvet.events or reply to any confirmation message. We will respond within 72 hours.
